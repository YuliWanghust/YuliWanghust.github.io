I am a third-year graduate student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, working with Prof. Harrison Bai. I am broadly interested in novel technologies and methods for quantitative biomedical imaging. Currently, my work focuses on using deep learning methods to capture information about the structure and function of living tissues.

I was previously a master student worked with Shiva Abbaszadeh at University of California, Santa Cruz and was a research assistant working with Craig Levin in the Molecular Imaging Instrumentation Laboratory (MIIL), at Stanford University and was a student affiliate at Universite PSL at the same time. I received a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2018.
